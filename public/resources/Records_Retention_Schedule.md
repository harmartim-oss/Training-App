# Records Retention Schedule

Template retention schedule for common record types with destruction procedures and legal requirements.

## Financial Records

| Record Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis | Disposition Method |
|-------------|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Accounts Payable/Receivable | 7 years | Tax Act | Secure shred |
| Bank Statements | 7 years | Tax Act | Secure shred |
| Budgets | 7 years | Municipal Act | Secure shred |
| Financial Statements (Annual) | Permanent | Municipal Act | Archive |
| Grant Records | 7 years after completion | Funding agreement | Secure shred |
| Payroll Records | 7 years | Employment Standards Act | Secure shred |
| Tax Returns | Permanent | Tax Act | Archive |

## Human Resources Records

| Record Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis | Disposition Method |
|-------------|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Employee Personnel Files | 7 years after termination | Employment Standards Act | Secure shred |
| Employment Applications | 1 year | Human Rights Code | Secure shred |
| Benefits Enrollment | Duration + 7 years | Pension Benefits Act | Secure shred |
| Workplace Investigations | 7 years after resolution | OHSA | Secure shred |
| Training Records | 7 years | OHSA | Secure shred |

## Legal and Compliance Records

| Record Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis | Disposition Method |
|-------------|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Contracts (Active) | Duration + 7 years | Limitations Act | Secure shred |
| Legal Opinions | Permanent | Municipal Act | Archive |
| Litigation Files | 7 years after resolution | Limitations Act | Secure shred |
| Insurance Policies | Duration + 7 years | Insurance Act | Secure shred |

## Technology and Information Records

|-------------|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| System Logs (Security) | 2 years minimum | MFIPPA | Secure deletion |
| Backup Tapes | 30-90 days rotating | Best practice | Secure wipe |
| Email (Corporate) | 2 years | MFIPPA | Secure deletion |
| Network Diagrams | Current + 2 years | Best practice | Secure deletion |

## Privacy and Access Records

| Record Type | Retention Period | Legal Basis | Disposition Method |
|-------------|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Access to Information Requests | 2 years | MFIPPA | Secure shred |
| Privacy Breach Records | 7 years | MFIPPA | Secure shred |
| Consent Forms | Duration + 7 years | PIPEDA/MFIPPA | Secure shred |

## Disposition Methods

### Secure Shred
- Cross-cut shredding (minimum DIN P-4)
- Certified destruction service
- Certificate of destruction retained

### Secure Deletion
- DOD 5220.22-M standard (3-pass minimum)
- Certified data wiping software
- Destruction log maintained

### Archive
- Transfer to archives
- Permanent preservation
- Accessible for historical research

## Record Hold

Records subject to:
- ☐ Active litigation
- ☐ Investigation
- ☐ Audit
- ☐ Freedom of Information request

Must be placed on LEGAL HOLD - Do not destroy.
